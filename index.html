About

Hi, I'm Amina, a digital experience designer and developer creating at the intersection of technology and human interaction.

My journey in tech began with a fascination for how digital interfaces shape our experiences. Today, I specialize in crafting immersive digital web applications that blend technical precision with thoughtful design. My work focuses on accessibility, user engagement, and innovative interaction patterns.

I’m passionate about open-source contributions, mentoring aspiring developers, and exploring emerging technologies like WebGL and AI-driven interfaces. When I'm not coding, you'll find me sketching UI designs, reading about HCI, or volunteering in tech education initiatives.
